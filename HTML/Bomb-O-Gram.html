BOMB-O-GRAMS

Support This Game

Use the clues you are given to uncover all the right panels without blowing yourself up or hitting the time limit! You know - kind of like in Minesweeper! Or Picross??? It's some type of hybrid, surely!

Ten levels of fun and incrementing challenge await you...


THE STORY WITH THIS ONE : Yeah so after witnessing the leaderboard of my last score-based game get HTML 'hacked' I figured I should go and make future projects of mine be moreso about the raw PROGRESSION and journey to a definitive end. Let me know if that worked out and if this one proves satisfying to complete! Or if its too easy even! I worry about that as well!!!

I also totally wanted to make this as just an excuse to give the 'remake' treatment to another old game of mine: 'BOOMBOARD!'. Game devs really DO only have couple repeating ideas don't they... Constant refinement is just too tempting!!!
