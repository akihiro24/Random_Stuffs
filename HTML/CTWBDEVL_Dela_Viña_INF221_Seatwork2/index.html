Welcome to my journey! Join me as I share these shots filled with joy and wonder as I travel through the lens of my camera. My blog captures the essence of each adventure, one frame at a time. As I wander our country, I'm not guided just by the beauty and admiration of our surroundings but also by the moments that triggers me to pull out my camera and press that shutter button.


Through this blog, I'm inviting all of you to visual journey with me, a journey filled with vibrant colors, serene landscapes, impressive structures, and unforgettable experiences. Each photograph we take tells a story we encounter, this blog of allows you to see the world through the eyes of my lens and mine.


This blog is not just a mere collection of images but also a way to tell stories and self-expression. Each time the shutter is pressed, a moment is captured, reflecting my unique perspective and my emotions that accompany it.


So, once again, join and explore this world with me through our lenses. Let's wander, wonder, and capture the beauty of our surroundings!

Calumbuyan Point | August 1, 2021

This journey happened after the pandemic. It was so much fun and exciting to see the outside world again, especially that we went to the beach because I love swimming. This day is my cousin's birthday, we had a blast enjoying the peaceful waves and cold sea water. There's so many things to see in this place, but this is the only scene I captured because at this moment I felt refreshed and glad to be seeing and enjoying outside again.

MOA by The Bay | March 2, 2022

In this day, I went out with 3 of my friends. We enjoyed window shopping and strolling around SM Mall of Asia. Even though there are too many people around at this time we still enjoyed the day as it is not that often that we go to malls together. We ate, played in the arcade, and landed lastly on MOA's by The Bay This made me captured the scene as I admire the sunset. I did not just admire the sunset, I also felt refreshed and calm seeing this view after a tiring day of strolling the mall.

Venice Grand Canal | July 9, 2023

We went to Venice Grand Canal to celebrate my sister's birthday. As we stroll around I found this astonishing view that's when I felt like I needed to take out my camera and capture the scene. After we finished strolling around the mall, we ate and then went inside "Wetpark Adventure Lagoon." We had so much fun as a lot of our relatives were present, especially me, since I brought my girlfriend with me making it more fun for me. This day ended tiring most of us but we did enjoy ourselves to the fullest inside the wetpark and that's what matters, to enjoy and make the most out of our day!

Diniwid Beach, Boracay | November 3, 2023

This trip with my family has got to be one of the most fun journey I've ever been in. We stayed at Boracay for 5 days and had lots of adventures. We visited different Islands, Beaches, etc. We also had our own shopping sprees (not so thrifty now are we?) well that's okay because its only one in a while we will ever be in this place. This place is called Diniwid Beach, there are a lot of structures that are abandoned here but is still accomodated with an amazing scenery. I captured this view with my cousin as the rest of our family decided to not venture and hike the path as it might be too dangerous for them. For me, this scene felt cool and calm which made me press my shutter as I admire the scenery's wonderful beauty.

Taal Basilica of St. Martin de Tours | December 25, 2023

We celebrated our Christmas on Calatagan, Batangas. At the same time, it's also my cousin's birthday. This Christmas day was too hot and tiring but still we won't ever miss visiting a church to pray and show our faith to Jesus. This church has its own playground where many children play, a lot of Shih-tzus were present here too, allowing our Shih-tzu to have her own fun with fellow dogs. I just felt capturing this scene as a memento because I don't think we'll be visiting this place again anytime soon.

That's it for a chapter of my blog of my journeys through the lenses of my camera.
I enjoyed sharing my experiences and wonderful shots with you. I hope I see you again soon!
